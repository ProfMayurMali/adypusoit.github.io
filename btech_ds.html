About Ajeenkya DY Patil University

Ajeenkya DY Patil University (ADYPU), Pune was established under the Maharashtra Govt. Act of 2015. It offers the best undergraduate and postgraduate programs in unique areas linked to the exemplary ‘ADYPU Student Experience’ supported by excellent facilities and highly motivated teaching staff. ADYPU, Pune, is a leading institution for vocational education with courses in rapidly expanding industries emphasizing on preparing its students for employment.

About Programs

Ajeenkya DY Patil University, Pune, is now inviting applications for the academic year 2023–2024 for B.Tech. admissions. Enrol in Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) programs in Data Science, Cloud Technology and Information Security and Mobile Application and Information Security at Ajeenkya DY Patil University in Pune. With these specializations, a B.Tech. degree opens up employment options in the global IT, technology, and industrial sectors.

These B.Tech. programs at Ajeenkya DY Patil University in Pune will offer students vocational experience from qualified instructors with experience in the relevant industry. After finishing the B.Tech. programs at Ajeenkya DY Patil University in Pune, students will have access to several employment opportunities that will help them advance their career. Additionally, ADYPU, Pune, provides merit-based scholarships for B.Tech. Fees for the academic year 2023–2024.
