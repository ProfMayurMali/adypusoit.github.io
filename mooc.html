Massive Open Online Courses – (MOOCs)

Basket of courses offered in CU with credit, to a student pursuing any program, to compliment his interest and Proficiency to enable a student to become a better learner. These Courses can be part of Core Courses, Elective Courses or Ability / Skill Enhancement Courses. (4 Courses for 3 year program and 6 courses for 4 year Program, applicable only for UG) 10% of the courses can be offered through MOOCs. These Courses can be part of Core Courses,Elective Courses or Ability / Skill Enhancement Courses. MOOCs can be offered in the following two modes

1. Credit Transfer:
The Student can take the approved course by the central MOOC Committee of the university, duly approved by the Head of the department for credit Transfer. Classes are to be conducted in flipped mode. Once the student successfully completed the MOOC Certification, Credit transfer to be initiated by Mentor. Assessment will be conducted in MOOC / CU Platform.

2. Blended Learning:
In blended learning mode, the course identified by the head of department and approved by the central team, the course learning will happen in the MOOC platform and assessments will be conducted through the University Examination process.
